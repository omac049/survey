# **Workforce Capability & Education Alignment Survey (2026)**

## **Introduction**

Organizations are navigating rapid changes in skills demand, workforce models, and learning pathways. This survey is part of a 2026 research initiative examining how organizations evaluate **workforce-aligned education**, applied degree pathways, and continuous learning in building workforce capability and organizational readiness.

Rather than focusing on hiring processes, this study explores:

* Workforce capability and skills alignment

* Perceptions of applied and professionally focused education

* The role of higher education in meeting evolving workforce needs

* Opportunities for education–industry collaboration

Responses are confidential and will be aggregated for analysis.  
Estimated completion time: **10–12 minutes**.
